Story of OneFlora - Building a Floral Website

So idea was to build Financial data website interface, but for Botanical data !!! voila...

Mandar Joshi 5 min. read


While it was a fun and entertaining journey to Identify plant species, I found that all the relevant info about the species is not aggregated on any single website. I had to refer thru multiple websites and books to understand all aspects of a species. Although the whole ID process so much relies on differentiation, there is no website that provides a feature to compare different floral and plant characters with photos. IDing is effectively a drilldown process, refining the filter criteria to reach to the desired species. Hardly any website provides such an intuitive search feature. Botanical language is another hurdle to decipher even for a seasoned player, there has to be some easy solution.


An a Finance Business Analyst, we often perform similar search and compare tasks for Financial data. For example - comparing two Equity stocks or Mutual Fund, searching for that less risky and more beneficial stock to invest.



It was an enthralling journey for about 6 month exploring the tech landscape and actually creating a product out of it. I can now call myself "Code Assembler"!!



It is indeed satisfying that I could crawl first few steps and reach the first mile. This is just a beginning!! here you go
